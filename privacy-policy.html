Privacy Policy for "Easy Marketo" Chrome Extension

Last updated: 18/08/2025

Thank you for choosing to use the "Easy Marketo" Chrome Extension. This privacy policy is intended to inform you about how we handle any information accessed or processed by this extension. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting it.

1. Information Collected

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information from your browser or your device. The extension operates solely on your local machine, and the data it accesses is used only to enhance your browsing experience.

2. How the Extension Works

The "Easy Marketo" extension is designed to:

  • Retrieve specific values, such as lpId and campaignId, from the web pages you visit.
  • Display those values as clickable links for your convenience.
  • These operations are executed locally on the user's browser and do not involve the transmission of data to any external servers.

3. Permissions Used

To provide its functionality, the extension requests the following permissions:

  • Active Tab: This is necessary for the extension to access the content of the currently active tab, identify the relevant values (lpId and campaignId), and display them as links.
  • Scripting: This permission allows the extension to execute JavaScript in the context of the web page to extract the necessary values dynamically. The extension only uses this functionality to parse the page and retrieve the relevant IDs.

4. Data Sharing

This extension does not:

  • Share any information with third parties.
  • Store any information outside your local device.
  • Collect any personally identifiable information (PII).

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. If changes are made, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Please review this privacy policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your data.
